Lessons Learned

Increase the risk

PM should have created a high attrition risk, based on PxP comments and DM´s recommendation in January 2023.

Be conservative

Whenever in doubt if the attrition risk should be raised or not, never hesitate to “over-react”. Higher risks mean higher visibility and more eyes to help with the resolution of the case.

Give the ticket better visibility

PM placed Attr ticket end date for a month ahead, leaving it untouched for too long without the proper updates. Attr tickets should be updated in a periodic basis.
